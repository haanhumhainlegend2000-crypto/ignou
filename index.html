about us

Our passion for design, your vision realized

Ethos Drafting Studio was established to close the critical gap between architectural vision and fabrication reality. We deliver high-precision technical documentation and visualization for firms that require institutional-grade authority and uncompromising accuracy.

Our philosophy is grounded in "shop-floor logic" because a drawing is only as valuable as its ability to be built efficiently and correctly. By integrating global architectural standards with fabrication-focused engineering expertise, we produce documentation that is field-ready, production-optimized, and execution-driven.
