01 / 04 Tile

Tile Shop Drawing

Tile shop drawings are detailed technical drawings that show the exact layout, pattern, dimensions, levels, and installation method of tiles before execution. They help avoid on-site errors by clearly defining joints, cuts, slopes, and coordination with other elements like fixtures and walls.

02 / 04 Terrazzo

Terrazzo Shop Drawing

Terrazzo is a composite flooring material made by embedding marble, quartz, or glass chips in cement or resin, then polishing it to a smooth finish. It is highly durable, low-maintenance, and offers a seamless, elegant look with customizable patterns and colors

03 / 04 Stone & Marble

Stone & Marble Shop Drawing

Natural stone flooring or cladding (like marble, granite, or limestone) brings strength and timeless aesthetics to a space. Each piece has unique textures and veins, and proper cutting, finishing, and installation are essential for durability and visual appeal.

04 / 04 As-Built

As-Built Tile & Stone Shop Drawings

As-built drawings are revised drawings created after construction, showing the actual dimensions and conditions of the completed project. They record all changes made during construction and are essential for future maintenance, renovations, and facility management.
